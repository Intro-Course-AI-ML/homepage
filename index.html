Intro to AI Course

A free Intro to AI Course for complete beginners

Many Lessons on
a Variety of AI Topics

Our course begins by covering the basics of AI and Machine Learning, and has lots of code and notebook examples. The course covers standard machine learning algorithms and progresses to building full-scale deep neural networks. By the end of the course, you will be able to build your own machine learning algorithms and deep neural networks and complete many different types of projects. At the end of the course, we have a variety of to explore, ranging from topics of image classification, time series analysis, and natural langauge processing.

Interactive Notebooks for
Learning Through Practice

Our course is more than just lectures. We focus on learning through walkthroughs and projects above all. Learning AI is more than just knowing concepts and theory, students must also learn how to implement those concepts in code, so we make it the core of our curriculum.
